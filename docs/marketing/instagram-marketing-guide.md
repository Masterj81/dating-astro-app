## Crisis Management

### Negative Comment Response
1. Respond quickly and empathetically
2. Move conversation to DM if needed
3. Offer solution or clarification
4. Never delete unless spam/abuse

**Template:**
```
We're sorry to hear that wasn't your experience! 💔 Could you DM us more details? We'd love to help make it right. ✨
```

### Trending Drama Response
- Don't engage with viral negativity
- Stay on-brand and positive
- Address legitimate concerns privately
- Focus on community building
