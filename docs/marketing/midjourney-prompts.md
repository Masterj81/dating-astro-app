## Global Settings

All prompts include these parameters:

| Parameter | Value | Purpose |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| `--ar 1:1` | Square | Instagram and Facebook feed optimal format |
| `--style raw` | Raw | Photographic realism, less Midjourney stylization |
| `--s 250` | Medium-high | Visual polish without over-stylization |
| `--no text words letters watermark` | Negative | Prevents any generated text or watermarks |

## Tips

- **Batch generation:** Run 4 prompts at a time in Midjourney for efficiency.
- **Variations:** After generating, use Vary (Subtle) for minor tweaks or Vary (Strong) for reinterpretations.
- **Upscaling:** Always upscale your final pick with U1-U4 before downloading for social media.
- **Seed consistency:** Note the `--seed` value of images you like and reuse it with other prompts for visual cohesion across your feed.
- **Color grading:** For extra brand consistency, run all final images through the same Lightroom preset (cool shadows, warm highlights, deep blacks).
