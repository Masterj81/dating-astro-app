## Community Engagement Strategies

### Response Templates

#### New Follower DM
```
Welcome to the AstroDating community! ✨

What's your Sun sign? We'd love to know who's joining us.

Ready to find your cosmic match? Download free: [link]
```

#### Comment Response - Sign Mention
```
A fellow [sign]! 🔥 We love that energy. Have you checked your compatibility matches yet?
```

#### Negative Comment Response
```
We appreciate your feedback! We're always working to improve. Feel free to DM us with specific suggestions — we read everything. 💫
```

### Engagement Tactics

**Daily:**
- Reply to ALL comments within 2 hours
- Like comments that mention their sign
- Ask follow-up questions to keep conversations going

**Weekly:**
- Host a "Sign of the Week" spotlight
- Share user-generated content (with permission)
- Go live for Q&A or horoscope readings

**Monthly:**
- Giveaway (premium subscription)
- Community poll for new features
- "Match of the Month" success story

### Crisis Response Plan

**Negative Review/Viral Complaint:**
1. Respond publicly with empathy within 1 hour
2. Move conversation to DM
3. Offer solution (refund, premium credit, direct support)
4. Follow up to ensure resolution
5. If resolved positively, ask if they'd update review

**Template:**
```
We're sorry to hear this wasn't your experience. That's not what we want for our users. Please DM us — we'd love to make this right. 💫
```

## Posting Schedule

### Optimal Times (US audiences)
- **Weekdays:** 1-4 PM EST
- **Weekends:** 12-1 PM EST
- **Best days:** Wednesday, Thursday, Friday

### Frequency
- Feed posts: 1x daily
- Stories: 2-3x daily
- Reels: 3-4x weekly
- Lives: 1x weekly (optional)

## Metrics to Track

### Awareness KPIs
- Page followers growth
- Post reach & impressions
- Video views (3-sec, 15-sec, complete)

### Engagement KPIs
- Engagement rate (likes + comments + shares / reach)
- Comment sentiment
- Share rate
- Save rate

### Conversion KPIs
- Link clicks
- App installs (via Facebook pixel/SDK)
- Cost per install (CPI)
- Return on ad spend (ROAS)

### Community KPIs
- Group member growth
- Active members (weekly)
- Posts per week by members
- Response time to messages
